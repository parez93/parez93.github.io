Short bio

My motto is: Homo sum, humani nihil a me alienum puto that means literally: I am a man, I consider nothing that is human alien to me.
I am a dynamic person, very curious and opened to new experiences that can enhance both me and my skills.

I completed my BSc in Computer Science (2015) and my MSc in Engineering and Computer Science (2017) at the Department of Computer Science of the University of Verona (Italy).
During my studies, I was elected 2 times as Student Representative and the accomplished achievements vary between bureaucratic interventions, detection of problems and critical issues, the creation of an efficient channel of communication between students and professors, and the organization of recreational and cultural events.
For two years I joined the Microsoft Student Parters program where I could organize technical events, be the speaker and provide support to students about Microsoft Technologies.

Currently I'm working in Reply as Consultant developing Java backend services (REST and SOAP) for applications in financial area and in 2018 I got OCA Java SE 8 Programmer I Certification provided by Oracle.
